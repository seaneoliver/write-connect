# Connect Goal Bucket Reference

Maps work types to their corresponding Connect goal buckets for use when classifying log entries during review drafting.

Replace the work types and goal bucket names below with your own. These are illustrative examples — adapt them to your role and Connect goals (see USER-CONFIG for your goal titles).

| Work type | Goal bucket |
|---|---|
| Program cycles, intake, training, decision criteria, stakeholder comms | Goal #2: [goal_2_title] |
| Tooling adoption, naming conventions, compliance, enablement | Goal #3: [goal_3_title] |
| Ethics training, values-based actions, policy compliance | Goal #1: [goal_1_title] |
| Cross-team influence, field enablement, org extension | Goal #2 or #3 depending on nature |
| AI measurement, executive KPI work, AI-related workstreams | Goal #3 or stand-alone |
| Broader org leadership work | Goal #2 or #3 — use judgment |
