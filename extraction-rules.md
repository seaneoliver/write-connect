# Extraction Rules

## Activity Test

Apply this gate before including any bullet:

- If you remove the verbs "attended," "supported," or "worked on" and the sentence no longer makes sense, it is describing activity, not impact.
- Activity bullets must be rewritten to state the outcome before inclusion.
- Every bullet must pass this test: What changed? Who benefited? Why did it matter?

## Impact Categories

Assign at least one impact category to each bullet:

- Risk reduced
- Revenue/capacity unlocked
- Delay avoided
- Optionality created

## Security, Quality, and AI Mapping

At least one reference to each is required in Section 1. Map them:

- **Security** — governance decision criteria, compliance controls, policy adherence behaviors
- **Quality** — zero hygiene issues, zero delivery gaps, documented frameworks, systematic tracking
- **AI** — AI-related workstreams, AI measurement tools, AI-enabled automation, AI program work
