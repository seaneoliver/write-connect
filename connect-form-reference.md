# Connect Form Reference

The form has two frames. All four sections are required.

## Reflect on the past

| Section | Char limit | Form guidance |
|---|---|---|
| 1. What results did you deliver, and how did you do it? | 6,000 | Include measurable outcomes, contributions to security, quality, and AI, and behaviors that demonstrate culture. |
| 2. Reflect on recent setbacks — what did you learn and how did you apply a growth mindset? | 1,000 | Specific examples of setback(s), what you learned, how you improved. |

## Plan for the future

| Section | Char limit | Form guidance |
|---|---|---|
| 3. Goals for the upcoming period | 1,200 per goal | Bold, measurable goals linked to business objectives. Security in at least one goal. |
| 4. How will your actions and behaviors help you reach your goals? | 1,000 | Reference: Grow with curiosity and adaptability / Collaborate and include others / Be bold, move fast, with purpose. |
